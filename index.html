Smart Features for Smart Citizens

Everything you need to make your city better, powered by cutting-edge AI

AI Image Verification

Our advanced AI analyzes every uploaded image to detect manipulations, ensuring only genuine reports reach authorities.

Auto Location Detection

Automatically pinpoints the exact location of reported issues using GPS, ensuring authorities can find and fix problems fast.

48-Hour Resolution

Every verified report triggers a 48-hour countdown for authorities. Track progress in real-time and hold them accountable.

Auto Authority Notification

Verified reports are automatically emailed to the concerned government department with all evidence and location data.

Real-Time Tracking

Secure & Anonymous

Your identity remains protected. Report issues without fear — we handle the communication with authorities on your behalf.
